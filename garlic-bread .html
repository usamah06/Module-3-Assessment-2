GARLIC BREAD

About our garlic bread:

Garlic bread is a beloved staple in many cuisines, particularly Italian, known for its irresistible aroma and comforting flavor. Typically made from a loaf of crusty bread-like baguette or ciabatta-it's slathered with a mixture of butter, fresh garlic, and herbs, then toasted to perfection. The result is a golden-brown exterior with a soft, buttery interior that melts in your mouth.
