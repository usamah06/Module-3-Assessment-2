TAMALE

About our Temale:

Tamales are a traditional Mexican dish made from masa (corn dough) that is filled with various ingredients, such as meats, cheeses, vegetables, or sweet fillings. The masa is wrapped in corn husks or banana leaves and then steamed, resulting in a savory or sweet treat. Tamales are often enjoyed during celebrations and holidays and are a staple in Mexican cuisine, showcasing regional variations and flavors.
