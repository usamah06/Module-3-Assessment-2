ENCHILADA

About our Enchilada:

Enchiladas are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of corn tortillas filled with various ingredients, such as meat, cheese, beans, or vegetables. The filled tortillas are rolled up and typically smothered in a rich chili sauce, then baked until heated through. Enchiladas can be garnished with toppings like sour cream, cheese, and chopped onions. They are a flavorful and versatile meal, often served with rice and beans, and come in many regional variations throughout Mexico.
