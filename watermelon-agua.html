WATERMELON AGUA FRESCA

About our Watermelon agua fresca:

Watermelon agua fresca is a refreshing Mexican drink made by blending ripe watermelon with water, a touch of sugar, and lime juice. It's typically served chilled, offering a sweet and hydrating flavor that's perfect for hot days. This vibrant beverage can also be garnished with mint or lime slices for an extra burst of freshness. Enjoy it as a thirst-quencher at picnics, barbecues, or simply on a warm afternoon!
