CHILES TOREADOS

About our Chiles toreados:

Chiles toreados are a popular Mexican dish featuring blistered, sautéed green chiles, typically jalapeños or serranos. They are usually cooked in oil until they are charred and tender, often with the addition of onions and garlic for extra flavor. Chiles toreados are served as a spicy condiment or side dish, adding a bold kick to meals, tacos, or as a topping for various dishes. Their smoky, savory profile makes them a favorite for those who enjoy a bit of heat in their cuisine.
