SOPAPILLA

About our Sopapilla:

Sopapillas are a popular dessert in Mexican and Southwestern cuisine, consisting of deep-fried pastries made from a simple dough. When cooked, they puff up to create a light, airy texture. Sopapillas are often served warm and can be drizzled with honey or dusted with powdered sugar. They can also be filled with savory ingredients or enjoyed as a side dish. Their sweet and flaky nature makes them a delightful treat for any occasion.
