HORCHATA

About our Horchata:

Horchata is a traditional Mexican beverage made from rice, milk, sugar, and cinnamon. Often served chilled, it has a creamy texture and a sweet, refreshing flavor. Variations may include additional ingredients like vanilla or almonds. Horchata is popular as a thirst-quenching drink, especially alongside spicy dishes, and is enjoyed for its unique taste and cultural significance in Mexican cuisine.
