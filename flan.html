CARAMEL FLAN

About our Caramel flan:

Caramel flan is a classic dessert in many Latin American and Spanish cuisines, characterized by its smooth, creamy texture and rich caramel flavor. Made with simple ingredients like eggs, sugar, milk, and vanilla, it features a layer of caramelized sugar on top that adds sweetness and a glossy finish. Flan is typically baked in a water bath to achieve its silky consistency, and it's often served chilled, making it a delightful and indulgent treat.
