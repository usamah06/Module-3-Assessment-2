TORTILA CHIPS & SALSA

About our Tortila chips & salsa:

Tortilla chips are crispy, fried corn chips that are a popular snack and appetizer in Mexican cuisine. Often served warm, they provide a crunchy base for dipping. Spicy salsa is a flavorful condiment made from tomatoes, onions, chili peppers, and various seasonings, adding a zesty kick. Together, tortilla chips and spicy salsa create a delicious combination that's perfect for sharing at parties or enjoying as a snack, offering a satisfying blend of crunch and heat.
