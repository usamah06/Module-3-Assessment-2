About our coyote:

Coyote dessert, also known as "coyote" or "coyote cookies," is a delightful treat that often features a combination of sweet flavors and textures. Typically made with ingredients like sweetened condensed milk, chocolate, nuts, and sometimes coconut, these desserts can be found in various forms, including bars or cookies. They are known for their rich taste and often incorporate a blend of flavors that reflect the fusion of traditional and contemporary dessert styles. Coyote desserts are popular at gatherings and celebrations, offering a satisfying sweet option for dessert lovers.
