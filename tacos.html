TACOS AL PASTOR

About our Tacos:

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. Common fillings include seasoned meats (like beef, chicken, or pork), beans, cheese, and vegetables, often topped with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream. Tacos can be served in soft or crispy tortillas and are enjoyed for their versatility, allowing for endless combinations of flavors and toppings. They are a beloved street food and a staple in Mexican cuisine, celebrated for their convenience and deliciousness.
